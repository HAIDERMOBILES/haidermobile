Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Please read our return policy carefully before claiming a return.

7 Days Replacement Warranty

We offer a 7-day checking warranty. If the product is defective or not as described, you can request a replacement within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for Return

  • The product must be in its original packaging.
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Proof of purchase (Order ID or Invoice) is required.

Non-Returnable Items

Items damaged due to misuse, electrical surges, or water damage are not eligible for return or replacement.
